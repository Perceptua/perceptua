A digital symphony. A global reading room. A gallery for all.

Perceptua is a growing collection, the product of my various creative & intellectual pursuits. At present, it contains essays, several short stories, & an audiovisual collage. With time, I hope to expand both its scope & its depth. In particular, I plan to augment this meagre collection with extensive research into the history & uses of mathematics. More broadly, I hope Perceptua will become a vast trove of historical & philosophical insights on the connections between knowledge & power, as well as a gallery for the art of a new cultural paradigm.
